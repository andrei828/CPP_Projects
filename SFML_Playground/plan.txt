Game:

    class members:
        map (20x23) -> 0 = empty | 1 = black | 2 = white
        bool turn -> 0 = black | 1 = white
    class functions:
        place_piece(Vector2i position) -> place piece and switch turn
        pass() -> switch turn
!       propose_draw() -> switch turn and ask player for draw (...)
        game_start() -> player 1 places 3 pieces on the board
        game() -> call game_start() then place_piece() or pass() or propose_draw() then check for end()
